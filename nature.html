Nature Adventures

Some of the cool nature I've seen.

I've been lucky enough to see some pretty cool nature in my life, here are some of my favorite photos.

A photo of me in a car mirror with the northern lights in the background.

It's always so crazy seeing the northern lights just watching them dance around the sky. Whenever they are out me and Rene like to drive somewhere with no light polution and just watch them for a while.

A photo of 4 bears in my back yard, 1 of the baby bears is standing up. This was so cool to see just outside my bedrrom window, now they come back without their mom and steal my garbage.


A beaver in a creek chewing on leaves. I took this photo on a hike in Wells BC, it was so cool to see a beaver in the wild.


2 different angles of the caribou mountain range outside of Wells BC and the second photo I took while driving through Mcbride. I really like mountains.

This is a moose me, Rene, and her dad saw just outside of Vanderhoof, we were going out to cut some fire wood early in the morning.
