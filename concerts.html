Concerts

Welcome to my concerts page!

Some of the concerts I've attended.

Over the past little while I have seen some pretty cool shows and these are some of my favorites.

Me and my girlfriend and a couple of friends saw Lights in Kelowna, it was so cool man and right after this we went on a camping trip in the west Kootneys.

Me, Rene, and my brother Gray went and saw Marina formally known as Marina and the Diammonds. We saw her at the orpheum theatre in Vancouver. Her singing is crazy good.

Me, Rene, and Gray went and saw Mother Mother and cavetown was the opening act. It was a really good show.



Me and my girlfriend Rene went and saw Geordie Keiffer at a club in Prince George and it was wild, that man and his producer had so much energy!


This concert was for my brother gray when he graduated, it was a lot of fun plus we were in Vancouver and it always cool seeing a big city.


Snotty Nose Rez Kids came to my town and it was really cool seeing indigenous artist here, was a lot of fun but i am sad we missed out on meeting them at the bar later that night.
